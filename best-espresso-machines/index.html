FAQ

What’s the easiest espresso machine for beginners?
If you want real espresso without early frustration, choose a forgiving semi-auto workflow (assisted tamping helps). If you want the simplest daily routine, a good super-automatic is usually easiest.
Is the grinder more important than the espresso machine?
Often yes. Espresso is sensitive to grind consistency. A capable grinder makes results repeatable and reduces bad shots.
Do capsule machines count as “espresso”?
They’re espresso-style and convenience-first. They can be excellent for consistent, fast drinks, but they don’t offer the same dialing control as semi-auto espresso.
How often should you descale?
It depends on your water hardness and machine type. Using filtered water helps. Follow the manufacturer’s guidance and adjust based on local water conditions.
