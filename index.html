FAQ

What type of espresso machine should I start with?
If you want the fastest routine with minimal cleanup, capsules are hard to beat. If you want to learn and improve your espresso over time, a semi-automatic machine (with a good grinder) is usually the best starting point. If you want one-touch milk drinks, consider a solid automatic.
Do I really need a grinder for espresso?
For semi-auto espresso, consistency depends heavily on grind quality. A capable grinder makes results repeatable and reduces wasted shots. That’s why we keep the grinder guide close to every machine recommendation.
Are capsule machines “worth it”?
If you value speed, consistency, and low maintenance, yes. The trade-off is ongoing capsule cost and less control. For many households, that’s an excellent deal.
